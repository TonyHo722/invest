## The "KQJ" Value Investing Method
The channel's approach relies heavily on "Left-Side Trading" (contrarian/value investing) during market panic. The core philosophy has two main principles:
1. **Risk First:** Do not invest unless there is at least a **50% upside potential** (with an ultimate goal of a 100% return).
2. **The "Big, Good, and Cheap" Framework (又大、又好、又便宜):**
   * **Big (又大):** Must be a global industry leader or monopoly.
   * **Good (又好):** Must be highly profitable and ideally distribute wealth to shareholders (e.g., paying steady dividends).
   * **Cheap (又便宜):** The stock price must be severely undervalued (Price < Intrinsic Value), completely abandoned by the market due to short-term panic.
